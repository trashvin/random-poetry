faqs
  • what is random poetry?
    • it is a web app that display random poems from contributors.
  • who are the contributors? .
    • anyone can be a contributor. just login to start submitting poems
  • can i submit poem written by someone else?
    • yes. just make sure the name of the writter is supplied on the authors name field.
  • i'm a writter and would like like to remove my article posted on the site without my persmission. what can i do?
    • email us the title of poem and will remove the poem after our investigation
  • do you earn from published poems?
    • no.
  • can i filter poems based on categories?
    • yes, you can by providing filter tags. the customization button can be found on the left part of the screen.
